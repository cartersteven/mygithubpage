How can I get a custom design made?
If you do not see a design in our product selection that suits you, we can create a custom design for you. We can also print your own designs on either blank DVDs or CDRs. Use the Contact Us page to send us more information. We will provide a price quote for you and provide instructions on how to send us your design.

What if the design I want is temporarily sold out?
You can order a design that is temporarily sold out. We typically reprint designs every other month. We will notify you by e-mail once the design you want is back in stock. If you send in an order form for a sold out design, we will ship the product to you as soon as it becomes available.

How can I use my blank DVDs and CDRs?
You can use the pre-designed blank DVDs or CDRs to back-up your data such as animation files, word-processing files, photographs, audio files, and video files. You can also send the disks to clients to create a more professional presentation.

What is the return policy?
Unfortunately, we do not accept returned merchandise.

How long will it take to get my disks?
Once we receive your order and payment, we ship your products out the same day through the postal service. You should expect to receive your products within 1-2 weeks.
